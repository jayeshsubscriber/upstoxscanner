## PART 2: FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

### External FAQs (Customer-Facing)

**Q1. What is Upstox Scanners?**
Upstox Scanners is a free web platform that helps stock traders and investors find trading opportunities using real-time technical scans. It includes 50+ pre-built scanners, a custom DIY screener builder, real-time alerts, and a community marketplace where traders share their screeners.

**Q2. Is it free to use?**
Yes. The core platform is free. You can browse pre-built scanners (top 5 results without login), build custom screeners, and join the marketplace. Logging in with your Upstox account unlocks full results, alerts, and trade execution. Upstox Plus subscribers get access to advanced indicators and exclusive scanner packs.

**Q3. Do I need an Upstox Demat account to use this?**
No. You can register with just your phone number/email and explore the platform. However, to trade directly from scan results, you will need an Upstox trading account.

**Q4. How is this different from ChartInk, Tickertape, or Screener.in?**
Three key differences:
- **Real broker integration**: When you find a stock, you can trade it immediately on Upstox — no platform-hopping.
- **Education built-in**: Every pre-built scanner explains the strategy, how to trade it, and risk management — not just a list of stocks.
- **Community + virality**: Share your screeners, build a following, earn badges. The marketplace is designed to grow with traders, not just serve them.

**Q5. How real-time is the data?**
Intraday scanners refresh live during market hours (every few minutes). EOD/swing scanners update at market close. All data is sourced from Upstox's live market feed.

**Q6. Can I create my own scanner?**
Yes. The DIY Screener lets you build multi-condition scanners using 100+ indicators across any timeframe. You can save screeners, run them on demand, and publish them to the community marketplace.

**Q7. How do alerts work?**
You can set any scanner (pre-built or custom) to trigger an alert. When stocks match your conditions, you are notified via WhatsApp, Email, browser push notification, or Upstox app push notification — in real time.

**Q8. What is the Screener Marketplace?**
The marketplace is a community library of scanners created by other traders. You can discover screeners by category (Trending, Editor's Choice, Most Used, Top Gainers from Scan), follow creators, copy and customize screeners, and share your own.

**Q9. What is included in the Plus Plan?**
Upstox Plus unlocks: advanced indicator pack (divergence, Ichimoku, Supertrend variants, CPR), exclusive pre-built scanner packs (institutional-grade swing + positional setups), unlimited alerts (Basic gets 3), and priority scan speed on larger universes.

**Q10. Can I trade directly from a scan result?**
Yes. On desktop, a trade ticket opens directly on the platform. On mobile, it deep-links into the Upstox app with the order pre-filled.

### Internal FAQs (Business & Product Hard Questions)

**Q1. Why build this on web instead of just improving in-app features?**
The Upstox mobile app serves existing users. This web platform has a fundamentally different objective: organic user acquisition. Every pre-built scanner page, every user-created screener, and every marketplace listing is an SEO-indexable URL. A trader googling "bullish engulfing stocks today" should land on our page — not ChartInk's. This is a distribution play, not just a feature.

**Q2. How does this actually drive UCC (Demat account) conversions?**
The funnel is: Discovery (SEO/social) → Hook (partial results, education) → Gate (login to see full results) → Lead captured → Nurture campaign → UCC open. The key insight is that a trader who has built and saved a custom screener on our platform has strong intent. They are far more likely to convert to a Demat account than a cold lead from a performance ad.

**Q3. What is the SEO strategy?**
Three layers:
1. **Pre-built scanner pages**: Each scanner has a unique, keyword-rich URL (e.g. `/scanners/bullish-engulfing-daily`, `/scanners/52-week-high-breakout`). These pages are server-rendered with live results, making them indexable.
2. **User marketplace pages**: Every published screener gets a public URL (e.g. `/screeners/@trader_xyz/golden-momentum-scanner`). Each one is a long-tail keyword opportunity.
3. **Education pages**: Each scanner's education module is a standalone article (e.g. "What is Consolidation Breakout and how to trade it") — targeting informational search intent.

**Q4. How do we prevent low-quality or spam screeners in the marketplace?**
Three-layer filter: (1) Minimum engagement threshold before a screener is publicly listed (creator must have run it at least 3 times), (2) Community flagging system, (3) Editorial team curates "Editor's Choice" and "Verified Creator" badges for quality control.

**Q5. How do we compete with ChartInk which has a 10-year head start?**
We do not compete on breadth alone. We win on three dimensions ChartInk cannot match: (a) **Broker integration** — one-click trade is impossible for a standalone tool, (b) **Education layer** — ChartInk shows stocks, we show stocks + teach you how to trade them, (c) **Real identity and trust** — our users are KYC-verified Upstox traders, giving social signals (likes, views, performance) higher credibility.

**Q6. What is the Plus Plan gating strategy?**
The goal is not to hide value — it is to create a clear upgrade moment. Basic users should feel the power of the platform, hit a specific wall (e.g. "You've used 3 of 3 alerts"), and see a concrete benefit to upgrading. We do NOT gate the scanner builder itself. We gate: advanced indicators, exclusive scanner packs, unlimited alerts, and scan on larger universes (All NSE). This creates natural upgrade triggers without alienating free users.

**Q7. What happens if a user publishes a scanner that gives bad trade signals?**
We add a clear disclaimer on all community screeners ("This screener is user-created. Past scanner matches are not investment advice."). Performance badges show historical hit rate transparently — a screener with a poor track record self-selects out of the trending section. We are not liable for user-created content as we do not endorse individual screeners.

**Q8. How does the real-time data pipeline work at scale?**
The platform uses Upstox's live WebSocket market feed. During market hours, scan conditions are evaluated on a rolling basis per stock as new ticks arrive. Alert triggers are processed server-side. Pre-built scanners with high traffic are cached and refreshed on a schedule (every 2-5 minutes) to avoid redundant computation.
