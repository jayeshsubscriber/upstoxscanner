## 2. INTRADAY EQUITY TRADERS

### 2A. Morning Scanners (Pre-market / First 15-30 min)
These are the bread-and-butter of every intraday trader. ChartInk's most popular public screeners revolve around these.

| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Gap Up Opening (>1%)** | Open > Previous High by 1%+ | Gap-and-go or gap-fill setups |
| **Gap Down Opening (<-1%)** | Open < Previous Low by 1%+ | Short or reversal plays |
| **Opening Range Breakout (ORB)** | Price breaks first 15-min high/low | Single most popular intraday strategy in India |
| **First 15-min Volume Shocker** | 1st candle volume > yesterday's full day avg | Massive early interest = momentum |
| **Open = High (Bearish)** | Open price = Day's high | Selling from the open — short candidates |
| **Open = Low (Bullish)** | Open price = Day's low | Buying from the open — long candidates |

### 2B. Momentum & Volume Scanners (Live/Intraday)
| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Volume Shockers (Live)** | Current volume > 5x avg 5-day volume | Most viewed screener on ChartInk |
| **VWAP Cross (Bullish)** | Price crossing above VWAP on 5-min chart | Institutional buying signal |
| **VWAP Rejection (Bearish)** | Price rejected at VWAP from below | Short setup |
| **Intraday RSI Oversold (<30)** | RSI on 15-min < 30 | Mean reversion bounce candidates |
| **Intraday RSI Overbought (>70)** | RSI on 15-min > 70 | Short or profit-booking alert |
| **Price > All Key EMAs (5/9/20)** | All short EMAs aligned bullish on 5-min | Strong intraday trend |
| **Nifty 500 Top Gainers/Losers** | Real-time sorted by % change | Quick momentum scan |

### 2C. Pattern-Based Scanners
| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Bullish Engulfing (15-min)** | Candlestick pattern on intraday chart | Reversal signal |
| **Inside Bar Breakout** | Today's range inside yesterday's — now breaking | NR7/NR4 strategy variants |
| **Pivot Point Breakout** | Price crossing R1/S1 levels | Classic intraday levels |

## 3. BTST (Buy Today, Sell Tomorrow) TRADERS
This is a HUGE category in India — not common globally, but very popular on ChartInk, TradingView India, and communities.

| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Closing Range Breakout (after 2:45 PM)** | Stock breaking day's high in last 30 min with volume | The classic BTST scan |
| **3:25 PM Momentum** | Rising price + rising volume in last 3 candles (15-min) | Run at 3:20–3:25 PM |
| **Above All Key EMAs at Close** | Closing price > 20/50/200 EMA + positive change% | Trend continuation overnight |
| **NR7 Breakout** | Narrowest range in 7 days, breaking out late in day | Compression → expansion |
| **Strong Close Near Day High** | Close within 5% of day high + volume > avg | Bullish overnight carry |

## 4. SWING / POSITIONAL TRADERS (2–20 days holding)

### 4A. Breakout & Momentum Screens
| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **52-Week High Breakout** | Price crossing 52-week high with volume | Most popular swing scan on Screener.in |
| **Consolidation Breakout** | Bollinger Band squeeze → expansion | Low vol → high vol transition |
| **Golden Crossover** | 50 DMA crosses above 200 DMA | Classic bullish trend signal |
| **Death Cross Alert** | 50 DMA crosses below 200 DMA | Bearish warning |
| **Darvas Box Breakout** | Within 10% of 52W high, 100%+ above 52W low, vol > 1L | Named scan on Screener.in — very popular |
| **Stocks Near All-Time High** | Within 5% of ATH | Momentum continuation |
| **Price-Volume Breakout** | Price up > 3% + Volume > 2x avg | Confirmed breakout |
| **Moving Average Bounce** | Price touching 50-EMA from above + bouncing | Pullback entry in uptrend |

### 4B. Mean Reversion / Oversold Screens
| Screener Name | Logic | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **RSI Oversold (Daily < 30)** | RSI on daily chart below 30 | Bounce candidates |
| **200 DMA Support Zone** | Price within 2% of 200 DMA from above | Key support test |
| **Price Below Lower Bollinger Band** | Oversold on BB | Mean reversion trade |
| **Bullish Divergence (RSI)** | Price making lower low, RSI making higher low | Reversal signal |

### 4C. Sector & Relative Strength

### Key Differentiators to Build

1. **Time-Aware Scanners**: BTST scan activates at 3:00 PM, ORB scan activates at 9:30 AM, Pre-market scan at 9:00 AM. No other Indian broker does this well.

2. **"Why This Stock?"**: For each result, show a 1-line reason — "Volume 8x above average" or "OI increased 23% with price up 4%". This is what ChartInk and Sensibull users love.

3. **One-Click to Trade**: Every screener result should have an instant Buy/Sell button. This is Upstox's natural advantage as a broker.

4. **Screener Alerts**: "Notify me when any stock hits this screener." ChartInk charges for this. Offering it free is a moat.

5. **Community Screens**: Let users create, share, and upvote screeners (like Screener.in's public screens). The most popular user-created screens become viral discovery tools.

6. **Usage Data (from Tickertape's public numbers)**:
   - Profitable Small Caps: 114k+ users
   - High-Volume Momentum (Intraday): 97k+ users
   - FII Accumulation: 76k+ users
   - Undervalued + Strong Fundamentals: 56k+ users
   - Dividend Gems: 34k+ users
   - Momentum Breakout: 25k+ users

   → Prioritize building these six first.
